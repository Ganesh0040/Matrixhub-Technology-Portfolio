Android Application Development

Android is the most widely used mobile OS. Android powers hundreds of millions of mobile devices today. It is a world-class platform for developing apps and games for Android users with an open marketplace for distributing them instantly. With the Android we can develop apps, games, and other digital content. Android is flexible and gives you tools for various great looking and functioning apps that look take advantage of the hardware capabilities available on each device. Due to its flexibility its automatically adapts your UI to look perfect on each device.

The main advantage Android has over other popular OS such as iOS is that it has an Open marketplace for distributing your apps. You can distribute your android apps to all markets and devices or focus only on specific segments, or devices. It is a huge market to target for selling and distributing Android apps, when you publish an app on Google Play, you arrive at the huge installed base of Android. You have control of the pricing for your apps and in-app products and can fix or change prices in any supported currency at any time.
