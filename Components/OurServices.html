Business Email Hosting Services

All businesses need an email address that can be accessed quickly, securely and reliably with enough storage to keep messages from bouncing. However, some companies only need a single address while other businesses require a scalable email solution. For that reason, we looked at a range of options that can serve anyone from the sole proprietor wanting a free address to a growing business with multiple employees. The criteria we used to evaluate each of these business email providers include:

Price : We compared the cost of basic email service, as well as the cost of paid upgrades or any other service fees
Ease of use : We reviewed user interfaces like webmail, administrative set-up requirements, and user reviews
Available storage : Each service’s policies with regard to email attachment size and general data storage was reviewed
Security & accessibility : We looked at how each service protects your data, their claims regarding their reliability of service as well as how easy it is to access your messages on a mobile device, desktop or browser-based client
Spam & virus protection : Each product’s built-in spam and virus protection was compared
Access to apps : We evaluated other communication and collaboration tools included with email hosting, which might lessen a business’ reliance on emails alone like Google Docs, Microsoft Word, Skype or instant messaging
System Integration : Each service’s ability to integrate with third-party apps and other office productivity tools was reviewed
Scalability : We compared the number of email addresses each service provided and if they included features like email aliases
Customer support: We looked at each service’s customer support channels such as phone, email, and live chat as well as hours of availability
