About Us

Overview

MatrixHub Technology provides Software and IT –enabled services & solutions aimed to give you more from your technology investments. With a rich and varied experience in providing offshore software development, strong project management capabilities and stringent quality standards. We develop solutions that give your business an edge over your competitors. We specialize in software product development, Mobile apps development, software testing, Cloud computing, e-Business solutions and Information Technology. Our customer-centric approach comes from understanding that our clients need more than technology professionals. We offer a strong team of developers in common and niche skill areas. Our offshore team works closely with locally situated project managers as they work to complete projects within time and budget.

Expertise stems from experience in developing software solutions in various verticals including Healthcare, Banking Services, Education, and Media & Entertainment. We currently provide services to a wide spectrum of clients ranging from startups to Fortune companies. We are actively engaged in Government projects.

As we move towards our goal of being a Professional Software Development Company in India, we will support research on a global scale. MatrixHub’s mission is to boost a dynamic of digital innovation that balances economic performance and human development.
